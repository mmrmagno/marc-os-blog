A music library manager built in Go. Downloads from Spotify and YouTube, organizes your collection into a clean folder structure, and syncs to a Rockbox iPod.

Inspired by [podlib](https://github.com/mikeshootzz/podlib).

Available on the [AUR](https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/mangolib-bin) for Arch Linux.
